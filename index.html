ei stands for environment & innovation. We are a boutique property development and architecture firm based in Kochi.We delight in creating enriching experiences through thoughtfully designed context sensitive spaces.

As a property development firm, our focus is on building unique exciting spaces in exotic locations that reconnect people with nature and create a true sense of well-being and joy. Our spaces are cherished for their "organic" architecture, unpretenious design, extensive use of 'non-manufactured' materials, connection with the outdoors and most importantly for being an expression of the owner's dreams.

The most popular development projects by our firm include Winterlake,Ooty, a 45 acre private paradise featured in the Architectural Digest and Anugraha Srishti in Kochi, a unique Holistic Lifestyle Commune. Our architectural and turn key projects include celebrity homes, boutique resorts, commercial establishments and even wellness centres.

We are an incredible working team and a close-knit family. We take pride in our skills, are passionate about our work and always learning from each other.
